BREAKFAST OPTIONS

Option 1: Ful Medames (فول مدمس)

Ingredients: ½ cup cooked fava beans (100g), 1 tsp olive oil (5g), ½ lemon juice, garlic, cumin, 1 small tomato (50g), parsley.

Macros: Calories: 180 | Protein: 9g | Fat: 4g | Carbs: 25g | Fiber: 8g

Why? High in fiber and plant-based protein, keeps you full for hours. Rich in iron and antioxidants.

Option 2: Oatmeal with Milk, Peanut Butter & Banana

Ingredients: 100g oats (dry weight), 100ml whole milk, 20g peanut butter, 1 medium banana (~120g).

Macros: Calories: 580 | Protein: 20g | Fat: 20g | Carbs: 85g | Fiber: 10g

Why? High in complex carbs (slow energy release). Healthy fats from peanut butter. Good fiber content for digestion.

Option 3: Scrambled Eggs with Olive Oil, Whole Wheat Bread & Veggies

Ingredients: 4 whole eggs, 1 tbsp (15ml) olive oil, 1 slice whole wheat bread (~30g), veggies (e.g., spinach, tomatoes, onions – ~50g).

Macros: Calories: 520 | Protein: 28g | Fat: 35g | Carbs: 20g | Fiber: 4g

Why? High in protein (great for muscle maintenance). Healthy fats from eggs and olive oil. Low-carb if bread is limited.

Option 4: Labneh with Veggies & Whole Wheat Bread

Ingredients: 50g labneh (strained yogurt), 1 slice whole wheat bread (~30g), veggies (cucumber, tomatoes, olives – ~50g).

Macros: Calories: 250 | Protein: 12g | Fat: 10g | Carbs: 25g | Fiber: 4g

Why? Probiotic-rich labneh supports gut health. Balanced macros for a light meal.
